Green personality style

Green personality types are said to be the most common in the world, they are characterised by co-operation, sincere recognition, and chances to be of help.

Challenges

  • Sticking to long-term routines
  • Focusing on the details
  • Showing patience with people and processes
  • Working with limited authority and autonomy

Goals

  • Taking on new challenges and opportunitues
  • Deligating work and making decisions
  • Creating tangible and measurable results
  • Independance from unwanted management

Annoyments

  • Waiting for peers to catch up on their tasks
  • Having little control over work pace and enviroment
  • Having their opinions on matters disregarded
  • Not getting credit for the work they have done
