Green personality style

Green personality types are said to be the most common in the world, they are characterised by co-operation, sincere recognition, and chances to be of help. They emphasize on giving support, collaboration, receiving support, whilst maintaining clear stability both for both themselves and their relations. Others would illustrate green types as tranquil, stoic, reliable, conscious, steady, and dependable.

Challenges

  • Confronting others
  • Taking feedback and critisim objectively
  • Quick adaption and multi-tasking
  • Being direct and decisive

Goals

  • Supporting the group to accomplish tasks
  • Promoting teamwork and cooperation
  • Keeping the status quo as it is without any changes
  • Pursue understanding and cohesion

Annoyments

  • Constantly working on a dense timeline
  • Making decisions in place of others without their input
  • Working alone or competing alone
  • Assigned tasks with vauge instructions
